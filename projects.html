Contacts

Cooling System Designer 

I have joined Lehigh University's FSAE racing team during my freshman year. I worked on the cooling system of LR-21 (FSAE competition entry of 2021). Using Lavic's correlation and engine data (RPM, Torque, Displacement, and power from dyno testing), the heat dissipation rate was calculated. By utilizing epsilon-NTU method and experimental effectiveness of 0.3, the minimum required heat-exchanger area was calculated. Similarly, by targeting a 20 degrees Celsius temperature difference, radiator fan was sized to 400 CFM. Furthermore, aerodynamic side-pod was designed and multiple inlet/outlet sizing iterations were tested on Solidworks flow simulation.  

Aerodynamics Lead

As aerodynamics lead this year, I am working on the design and testing of various aerodynamic devices of LR-22 (FSAE entry of 2022). We will be implementing the new aerodynamic devices such as front/rear wings in addition to existing aerodynamic devices like undertray, nosecone, and side-pods. Using Star CCM+, the aerodynamic devices are being tested to optimize aerodynamic efficiency and reduce drag.

Team Lead/Mechanical Systems Designer

After participating as a research fellow of the Mountaintop project, Autonomous Driving for Underwater Drones, I became the team lead of the student-driven group, Lehigh Underwater Robotics (LUR). As a team lead, I oversee the design, development, and testing of a new Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) for Robosub 2022, an international underwater robotics competition. Additionally, I work with our Mechanical team in the design and validation of AUV hull, frame, gripper, and torpedo launch systems.

A SEDS USA Chapter of Lehigh University

Co-founder & President 

I am a cofounder & president of Lehigh University Space Initiative, a SEDS USA chapter of Lehigh University that promotes engagement in space exploration through various engineering project. Currently, our team is working on a Mars like rover, LUCY V1.0 for the University Rover Challenge (URC) 2022/2023. In addition, URC 2022, our team is also working on a concept design of Portable Utility Pallet for lunar exploration as an entry for NASA RASC-AL 2022 challenge. I mainly work with chassis & drive sub-team in addition to overall system integration and validation. Additionally, I have designed a prototype version of our HawkSat CubeSat to demonstrate an attitude control and determination system for a zero-g flight experiment.
