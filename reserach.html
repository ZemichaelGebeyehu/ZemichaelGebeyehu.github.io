Undergraduate Research Assistant 

Unsteady Flow Laboratory : Feb 2022 - Present
Faculty Advisor: Prof. Moored  
As an undergraduate researcher in Moored's Lab, I mainly work on the study of fluid-meditated behavior and self-organization of fish schools. In order to study such phenomenon, we utilized two robotic fish in different formations under a water channel.  



Undergraduate Researcher 

Unsteady Flow Laboratory/Aerospace Controls Lab: Feb 2021 - Present  

Starting from the spring semester of my first-year, I joined the Unsteady Flow Laboratory/Aerospace Controls Lab to work on the design and testing of an autorotating vehicle, under the supervision of Prof. Keith Moored. My research was focused on optimizing thrust/drag of the vehicle through various closed-return and vertical wind tunnel testing. I worked on the design of experimental apparatus, data acquisition setup, and LabView interface of our experiments. Additionally, I am also working on autonomous flight/control algorithms, implementation, and development of Ground Control Software (GCS) of the system.  

Research Fellow

Mountaintop Summer Research Program : June 2021 - Aug 2021
Faculty Advisor: Prof. Rosa Zheng 

As a research fellow of the Autonomous Driving for Underwater Drones project, I worked on the hardware and software requirements of changing a heavy class Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) to Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV). By utilizing MAV link protocol and ArduSub, we have been able to develop various control and computer vision algorithms for object detection and collision avoidance. We have also conducted various simulations of our code using Qgroundcontrol and BlueSim environment in addition to on-site validation & testing.

Research Extern 

    United Nations International Labor Organization/Lehigh University: January 2021
    As a research extern, I have worked in a team with people from various parts of the world to investigate the technical feasibility of AI substitution of harvesting jobs in the United States during the COVID-19 pandemic. Our team utilized O*Net and RLI data sets as well as case study of a current technologies and robotics companies involved. We have drafted policy recommendations for US government and employers in the harvesting industry.
